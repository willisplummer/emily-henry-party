Lodging Options

Stewart House

2 N Water St, Athens, NY 12015 -- 5 min walk
We're almost hesitant to suggest it because it only has 9 rooms and will book up super quickly, but if you can, grab a room here! It's right in town, right on the water and super beautiful. If you miss out on a room, don't worry, you can join us for drinks at their River Garden on Friday night and we encourage you to grab dinner inside at the beautiful bar or dining room afterward.

Camptown

810 Co Rd 23B, Leeds, NY 12451 -- 10 min drive
Super cute motel and cabin resort. You can book either a private cabin or room, and they have a really nice pool & pool bar as well. The attached restaurant, Casa Susanna, serves delicious Oaxacan food and is very cozy.

voco James Newbury Hudson Valley by IHG

60 S River St, Coxsackie, NY 12051 -- 10 min drive
This is a larger modern hotel with all the amenities: spa, fitness center, accessible rooms, multiple bars & restaurants onsite.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will there really be swimming?

Weather permitting, yes! We have a 4' above ground pool, so no one will be doing laps, but we'll have pool floats and encourage guests to enjoy one of our favorite parts of the house. If you're planning to swim, please bring your own towel and a change of clothes to keep yourself comfortable.

Can I get there late? What if I have to leave early?

No problem! Feel free to participate in the parts of the celebration that you can. We'll be so glad to see you :)

Can I bring a gift?

If you really want to, sure! But, there's absolutely no pressure to bring a gift and we do not have a registry. We are fortunate to already have a well-stocked home and full life. Your presence at our celebration means the world to us!

What should I wear?

Whatever you want! Bring a swimsuit if you want to enjoy the pool, and otherwise whatever makes you feel comfortable and celebratory. We'll be outside so it will likely be warm. And we'll be on a lawn, so skip the stilettos.

What if it rains?

Fingers crossed it doesn't! But just in case, we'll have a tent outside, and can move the party inside if needed.
