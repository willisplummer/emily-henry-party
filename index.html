Our Story

Emily and Henry first met at a coffee shop in Portland, where a mix-up with their orders led to an unexpected conversation that lasted hours. What started as a chance encounter over lattes turned into weekend hikes in Forest Park, exploring Portland's food scene, and discovering a shared love for live music and adventure.

Three years later, Henry proposed during a sunrise hike at Mount Hood, with the perfect view of the Oregon landscape they both love. Now, they're excited to celebrate their next chapter with all the people who mean the most to them.
